As you explore the tunnels, you start to notice something terrifying:
The walls are pulsing, as if they are breathing.

Doors that were open before are now sealed shut.

The paintings are changing, showing you in them—even though you’ve never been here before.

Then you hear the whisper:
"You are not a visitor. You are the next Blackwood."

The mansion is not haunted—it is alive, and it wants to make you its new master.

If you stay, you will inherit the curse and rule the ghosts.

(Ending: "The New Lord of Blackwood")
