When you pick up the golden locket, the walls start bleeding, and a shadowy figure emerges from the portraits.

The ghost of Lord Blackwood laughs coldly:
"You fool… The locket was never the key to freedom. It was the key to your doom."

The locket burns your skin, and you realize it's bonding to you.

You try to drop it, but it won’t let go.

Your vision blurs, and suddenly you are standing inside one of the portraits, trapped forever.
