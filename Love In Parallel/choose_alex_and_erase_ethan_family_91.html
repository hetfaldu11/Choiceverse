You take a deep breath and look at Alex. The memories of your childhood, the laughter, and the countless moments you’ve shared feel more real than anything else. “I choose you,” you whisper. You should feel whole, but something is missing. One night, while unpacking an old box, you find a wedding invitation with a blurred name. The date is for a wedding that never happened. Your heart clenches, but the memory is gone. Ending Theme: A bittersweet love—happily together, but with an unshakable feeling of something lost.
