It’s your wedding day with Ethan/Emily.

The venue is perfect, the guests are smiling, and everything feels straight out of a dream.
But something is off.

Just before you walk down the aisle, an anonymous note is slipped into your hands:
"Don't say 'I do.' If you do, you will never wake up."

Then, for just a moment, you see Ethan/Emily's face flicker—as if they’re not who they claim to be.

  • Say "I do" anyway and
    embrace your love
  • Refuse to marry and
    demand the truth
