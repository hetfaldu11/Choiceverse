Story Concept:

You are Dr. Elias Carter,
a brilliant but reckless physicist who has been working on a classified government experiment known as Project Paradox.

It aims to unlock a new dimension of existence, where reality itself can be rewritten.

But during the final test, something goes wrong. The machine overloads, and you are pulled into a world where

time, space, and logic no longer follow the rules you know.

Now, you must navigate through fractured realities, make choices that shape the fate of entire worlds, and uncover the true purpose behind Project Paradox.

Will you escape? Or will you become part of the paradox forever?
