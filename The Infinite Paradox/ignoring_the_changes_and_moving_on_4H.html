You force yourself to ignore the oddities and try to live as if everything is normal.

But as the days pass, you begin to notice a terrifying pattern:
People forget you exist. Your own family doesn’t recognize you.

Your home, job, and belongings vanish—as if they were never real.

Eventually, you can’t even see your own reflection.

A final message appears on your phone, from an unknown sender:
"You are being erased. Turn back while you still can."

  • Try to fight against
    your own erasure
  • Surrender and
    accept your fate
