His lips move faster. Then, you hear a voice—but it’s in your head.
"You don’t belong here. You were never supposed to wake up."

The train jerks violently. The voice grows weaker:
"Find the briefcase… before IT finds you."

The man lets go, collapsing in his seat like a lifeless doll.

Do you:

  • Go look for the briefcase?

  • Stay in your seat and
    wait for answers?
