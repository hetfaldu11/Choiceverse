Your fingers tremble as you click it open.

Inside is a single train ticket with today’s date.

But the destination is blank.

There’s also a mirror inside.

You glance at your reflection… and see someone else’s face.

Your real memories flood back—you were in an accident. You were never supposed to wake up.

The train is a limbo. A test.You only get one chance to leave.

Do you:

  • Take the ticket and head
    to the next compartment?
  • Smash the mirror?
