You turn to the man beside you.
“Hey… where’s this train going?”

He slowly turns his head. His lips move, but there’s no sound.
His eyes look empty—like glass marbles.
You lean closer.

Suddenly, he grabs your wrist. His grip is ice-cold.

  • Pull away and run?

  • Let him speak maybe
    he knows something?
