You track down Dr. Julian Voss, the only survivor of Project Echo.

His file says he died eight years ago—yet he’s alive, and he remembers two different lives.

Key Revelations:

The vault wasn’t stolen—it was placed in an alternate timeline.

Project Echo wasn’t shut down—it’s still running, and you are now inside its latest simulation.

  • Try to escape the simulation
  • Use Dr. Voss’s knowledge to control the timeline shift

Twist: Dr. Voss gives you coordinates—but they lead to a place that doesn’t exist.
